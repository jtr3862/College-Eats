Types of Diets

Low-Carb
- The low-carb diet is perfect for people who need to lose weight, optimize health
Keto
- It involves drastically reducing carbohydrate intake and replacing it with fat. This reduction in carbs puts your body into a metabolic state called ketosis.
Gluten-Free
- The gluten-free diet is essential for people who are intolerant to gluten, a protein that is found in wheat, rye, and barley.
Paleo
- Predominant eating of foods presumed to have been available to humans during the Paleolithic era

Why diet?

Following a diet during college can aid you in knowing what to buy, and knowing what to have for your meals. There is also the obvious benefits that dieting brings.
